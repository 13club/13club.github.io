Penguin Solitaire

Move all the cards to the foundations on the left, by building up in suit starting from the rank of the first card dealt wrapping from King to Ace. Use the 'free cells' on top to temporarily store a card. On the tableau build down in suit. Move groups of cards if they are in sequence down in suit. Empty spaces can be filled only by cards one rank below the starting card of the foundations.
