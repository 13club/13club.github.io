Pesten

Be the first player to get rid of all your cards. When on turn either play a legal card or draw a card from the stock. A legal card matches the top card on the discard pile in rank or in suit. A Jack may be played on any card and the player on turn must nominate a suit. When a 2 is played you must play another 2 or take 2. When an 8 is played the next player has to skip a turn. When a 7 is played you can play another card and when a Joker is played you need to play a Joker or take 5.
